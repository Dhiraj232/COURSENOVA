    India's Trusted Peer-to-Peer Used Books Marketplace for Students

    Welcome to the COURSENOVA Used Books Marketplace — a student-first, zero-fee platform designed to help college students, school learners, and competitive exam aspirants across India buy, sell, and exchange used textbooks and study materials directly with each other. Whether you are looking for an engineering textbook, a UPSC preparation guide, a CBSE Class 12 science book, or a medical entrance reference, you will find it here at a fraction of its original cost.

    The rising cost of new academic books is a significant burden for Indian students. A single semester's worth of textbooks can cost thousands of rupees — money that families struggle to allocate year after year. COURSENOVA's marketplace was built to solve this problem. By connecting buyers and sellers directly, we eliminate the middleman entirely, ensuring that the savings go straight to you.

    Why Choose COURSENOVA for Used Books?

    Unlike traditional second-hand bookstores where you get a fraction of your book's value, our platform lets you set your own price and negotiate directly with buyers. Sellers keep 100% of what they earn — there are no listing fees, no commission cuts, and no hidden charges. For buyers, this means access to quality used books at honest prices, often 40–70% cheaper than buying new.

    Our marketplace is tightly integrated with the COURSENOVA educational ecosystem. When you're not hunting for books, you can explore our premium online courses, join the vibrant student community, or sharpen your exam readiness with free mock tests. Learn more about our mission on the About COURSENOVA page or return to the homepage.

    How to Sell Your Old Books

    Selling is as simple as clicking the "Sell Your Book" button, filling in your book's title, author, condition, price, and uploading a clear photo. Your listing goes live instantly and is visible to thousands of students across India. When a buyer contacts you, coordinate directly via the provided contact number — no involvement from COURSENOVA needed.

    We support books across all major academic disciplines including Engineering (CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil), Medical and Allied Health Sciences, Commerce and MBA, Arts and Humanities, School (Class 1–12), and all major Competitive Exams such as JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, SSC, and Banking exams.

    How the COURSENOVA Book Marketplace Works

    1. Create Your Account
