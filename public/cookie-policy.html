    Cookie Policy

    Last Updated: July 03, 2026

    This Cookie Policy explains how CourseNova uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://coursenova.in.

    1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit websites. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, function more efficiently, and provide analytical reporting information.

    2. Why We Use Cookies

    We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons:

    • Essential Cookies: These are strictly required to provide you with services available through our website (e.g. keeping you logged into your dashboard, facilitating secure checkout processing).
    • Analytics and Performance Cookies: These help us understand how users navigate our platform, allowing us to fix issues, track page response times, and improve the general learning experience.
    • Functional Cookies: These remember details such as your language preferences, theme selections, or progress status inside mock tests.

    3. How to Manage Cookies

    You have the right to accept or decline cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject essential cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some features and secure areas (like course player and checkouts) may be restricted.
