    Pricing Policy

    Last Updated: July 03, 2026

    At CourseNova, we believe in fair, transparent, and accessible pricing for quality education. This Pricing Policy details how our pricing structures, billing cycles, promotional offers, and taxes are managed across our online platform.

    1. Standard Pricing Structures

    All product prices are clearly displayed on the platform store and checkout forms. We offer the following products:

    • Individual Courses: One-time fee structure for lifetime access to video modules, syllabus notes, and progress trackers.
    • Mock Test Hub Packs: Tiered pricing depending on the examination category (e.g. NEET, SSC, railway exams).
    • Premium Notes/PDFs: Fixed pricing per subject or chapter block in our Store.

    2. Taxes and Additional Fees

    We believe in honest checkout terms. All prices shown in our listing catalogue are exclusive or inclusive of applicable Indian taxes (GST) depending on standard billing guidelines:

    The total pricing breakdown (including Base price, Taxes/GST, and Discounts) is fully items-detailed on the Checkout Page before you complete any secure payment gateway interaction.

    3. Promotional Offers & Coupons

    CourseNova may occasionally run promotional campaigns or distribute coupon codes to students. These terms apply:

    • Coupons must be applied at checkout before clicking the payment button to benefit from the discount. Retroactive discounts cannot be applied.
    • Coupons may have expiry dates, target limits, and are non-transferable or exchangeable for cash.
    • CourseNova reserves the right to modify or terminate pricing promotions without prior notice.

    4. Invoice Generation

    Upon successful payment confirmation, our platform automatically generates a detailed tax invoice receipt. This contains the billing details, base product value, applied discount, final paid amount, and tax components, which can be viewed or downloaded from your user dashboard at any time.
