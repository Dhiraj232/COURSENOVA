    Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: March 09, 2026

    At COURSENOVA, we prioritize the privacy and security of our students. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our educational platform.

    1. What Data We Collect

    We collect information to provide a personalized learning experience. This includes:

    • Identification Data: Name, email address, and profile picture (via Google Sign-In).
    • Academic Information: College name, branch, and current year of study.
    • Learning Progress: Course enrollments, lesson completion status, quiz scores, and earned certificates.
    • Technical Data: Device information, browser type, and interaction logs.

    2. How We Use Your Data

    The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

    • To provide and maintain our educational services.
    • To track your learning progress and generate certificates in your name.
    • To provide performance analytics and "weak topic" insights in your dashboard.
    • To enhance platform security and prevent unauthorized access.
    • To communicate important updates and educational content.

    3. Google Login Usage

    COURSENOVA uses Google OAuth 2.0 to simplify the login process. When you choose to "Continue with Google":

    We only access your public profile information (name and picture) and primary email address. We never access your contacts, files, or any other private data from your Google account.

    Authentication tokens are stored securely in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in between sessions.

    4. Security & Protection

    We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

    • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted.
    • Secure Storage: Sensitive user information is stored in secured, MongoDB-backed databases.
    • Zero Password Storage: Since we use Google Auth, we do not store your passwords on our servers.

    5. Cookies & Tracking

    We use cookies and local storage tokens for:

    • Authentication: To recognize you and maintain your secure session.
    • User Preferences: To remember settings like dark mode or your chosen college.
    • Analytics: To understand platform usage and improve our learning modules.

    6. Your Rights

    As a student on COURSENOVA, you have the right to:

    • View the personal information we hold about you via your Profile Settings.
    • Update your academic details at any time.
    • Request the deletion of your account and all associated learning data.

    7. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact our privacy team:
